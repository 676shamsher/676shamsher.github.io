Welcome

This is the official website of the Hashemite Sayyed Family of Hejaz — descendants of Banu Hashim, caretakers of the prophetic legacy, and steadfast defenders of Madinah’s noble traditions. Our ancestors served the Haramain for centuries with honor and dignity.

About Us

The Sayyed family traces its lineage directly to the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) through both Imam Hassan and Imam Hussain. The family never migrated despite changing empires and continues to reside in its ancestral palace in the heart of Madinah.
